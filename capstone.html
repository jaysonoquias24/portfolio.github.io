Capstone Project

TARA LARO: A 3D Animation Showcasing the Filipino Traditional Games

The researchers want to create a fusion of multimedia technology and traditional cultural games because they recognize the value of instilling sportsmanship and knowledge of Filipino traditional games in all Filipino children. The study will allow Filipino children to be more engaged in playing Filipino traditional games, how it is played, and the lessons they might learn while playing. The researchers have considered creating a 3D animation about how traditional Filipino games because today's generation of youth has become more technologically oriented in the aspect of games and still needs to remember the culture of Filipino traditional games. The project's main goal is to promote and bring back old-fashioned Filipino games. The traditional games played in many regions of the nation will be featured in the 3D animation. The website will include details on these games, including how to play them, their cultural significance in the Philippines, and the equipment needed to play them. It seeks to raise public awareness of the beauty and distinctiveness of Filipino traditional games.

Role: Lead Artist (Storyboard, Logo Design, Web Design) | Bring story settings to life through 3D modeling. Additionally, I develop websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.
