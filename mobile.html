Cookie Math Quiz

The math quiz game is an application intended to be use as a platform in honing mathematical skills of people of all ages as it offers interactive interface and a thrilling mathematical game that enhances critical thinking and problem-solving skills of a user. It would include four arithmetic quizzes for Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division. The game will also give users opportunities to explore fundamental number concepts, such as the counting sequence, one-to-one correspondence, and computation strategies, explore number combinations, place value, patterns, and other important mathematical concepts
