Linking Aid: EPDM Application (Emergency Preparedness and Disaster Mitigation)

In our Platform Technologies course, our team Hi-5ive has developed a mobile application called Linking Aid: EPDM (Emergency Preparedness and Disaster Mitigation). The focus of our project is to integrate various platforms, including Network, Human, Media, and Convergence, to create a comprehensive solution. This mobile app aims to streamline emergency preparedness and disaster mitigation efforts. With Linking Aid, users can access vital information and resources, fostering a more connected and responsive community during critical times. Our goal is to enhance overall preparedness and provide a user-friendly tool for better disaster management.
