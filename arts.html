Character Design & Digital Arts

I. Character Sheet

In our Design and Animation class, we're working on a cool project: creating a character that's more than just a drawing. We have to give it profiles, show it from different angles, capture its facial expressions, and even make it walk in an animation. It's not just about making it look good; it's about making it feel alive through movement and expression. It's a fun challenge that takes our design and animation skills to the next level!

II. Digital Arts

I discovered digital drawing in my senior year of high school in late 2019, inspired by my talented seatmates and friends. Seeing their skills, I asked them to teach me, and through their guidance, I learned a great deal. This journey taught me that to learn something, you must take the initiative—it won't come to you; you have to seek it out. Now, here are my latest works, a testament to the strides I've taken in my artistic journey.
